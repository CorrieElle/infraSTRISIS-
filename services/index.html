Crisis Infrastructure Sprint

The Sprint is a 4-6 week agent-assisted engagement where infrastructure automation handles structural drafting, tracking, and scheduling, while human oversight ensures strategic alignment, quality control, and direct client advocacy.

By Engagement Only. Sprint architecture, timelines, and pricing are custom-built based on the specific structural fractures identified during your Diagnostic Session. Engagements begin at $7,500.

Agent-Assisted Sprint Model: How It Works

We use automated agents to compile, draft, and track work, significantly shortening turnaround times and eliminating standard agency overhead.

Week 1: Intake & Diagnosis

Deep-dive kickoff call with human-led discovery, augmented by agent-driven research on compliance baselines, operational data, and ecosystem friction points.

Week 2: Crisis Map & Draft Playbook

Agents compile research and draft structured checklists, procedures, and playbook modules. The Steward synthesizes these outputs into a cohesive strategic draft.

Week 3: Client Review & Revisions

A collaborative, human-led session to review drafts, target friction points, align on immediate implementation priorities, and iterate on necessary workflows.

Weeks 4-6: Refinement & Handoff

Final playbook polish, delivery, and system handoff. Agents establish automated tracking pipelines and check-ins to monitor implementation milestones.

Human Touchpoints

  • Intake Kickoff Call: 90 minutes of dedicated, high-context discovery.
  • Mid-Sprint Alignment: 30 minutes of human-to-human check-in (optional, as needed).
  • Delivery & Handoff Call: 60 minutes of strategic walkthrough and execution training.
  • Steward Synthesis: Critical thinking, context-filtering, and quality assurance at every step.

Agent Touchpoints

  • Automated Weekly Progress Updates: Direct visibility into tasks without meetings.
  • Draft Deliverable Generation: Accelerated baseline procedures and playbook structures.
  • Implementation Tracking Dashboard: Real-time status views on critical actions and burndown.
  • Ecosystem Research: Real-time background queries on system policy, compliance, and structures.

90-Day Implementation Roadmap

We believe in accountability and visibility without constant, unnecessary human overhead. Our post-sprint roadmap is structured for sustainability:

  • Automated Milestone Tracking: Systematic check-ins at Days 30, 60, and 90 to track implementation progress.
  • Two Human Coaching Calls: Strategically scheduled at critical decision points or operational milestones.
  • Agent-Generated Progress Dashboards: Automated progress visualizations generated instantly when your team submits quick milestone updates.
  • Asynchronous Verification: Standard follow-ups are automated. Human synthesis and intervention are brought in only when a system block is flagged.
