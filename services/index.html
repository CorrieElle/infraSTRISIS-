Operations Signal Brief

The quality of the diagnostic is determined by the quality of the signal. Before the Audit call, you submit a structured brief that gives us a clear picture of what the organization is actually experiencing. The more precise your input, the more precise the map we build from it.

  • What's Not Working (3 pages max): What you're observing, what triggered the concern, what success looks like from where you sit
  • Org/System Structure (1 document): Who reports to whom - org chart, list, or system narrative
  • What You've Already Tried (1 page): Interventions attempted and what happened. This prevents us from recommending what's already been ruled out.
  • Optional enrichment: 2-minute video walkthrough + up to 3 supporting documents
Timing: Signal brief submitted 5 business days before your Audit call. We review it in full before the session so the diagnostic starts from your actual situation, not from scratch.
