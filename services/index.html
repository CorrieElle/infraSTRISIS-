Pre-Work Requirements (All Tiers)

  • Crisis Narrative (3 pages max): What's breaking, what triggered it, what success looks like
  • Org Structure (1 document): Who reports to whom (org chart, list, or narrative)
  • Attempted Solutions (1 page): What you've already tried and what happened
  • Optional: 2-minute video walkthrough + up to 3 supporting documents
Deadline: Pre-work due 5 business days before your scheduled Diagnostic call. Submitted materials are reviewed before the session to build your custom diagnostic agenda.
